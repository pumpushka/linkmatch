Hey guys,

We are thrilled and excited to share with you our product!

As a team of recruiters and developers we wanted to create not just A product, but something that each recruiter and sourcer could benefit from.

We were upset and demotivated by the amount of hours per day that were wasted on manual work of recruiters and sourcers.

As a result, we decided to optimize and automate the process as much as possible, so that you could focus only on the work that really matters.

LinkMatch is the first step out of a thousand that we are planning to take so that recruiting is better, simpler and more profitable for you.

We use it ourselves, others are loving it and it’s addictive. In a good way of course :)

Join us for this amazing experience that will forever change how recruiting and sourcing is done.
